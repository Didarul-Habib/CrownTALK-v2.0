1) CORE VALUE PROP
===============================================================================
- Problem: crypto influencer marketing often involves intermediaries, opaque pricing, delayed payouts and weak measurement.
- Solution: Kreators runs structured campaigns where brands launch briefs and budgets, creators join and produce content, and payouts stream directly in stablecoins on Base.
- Differentiation: emphasizes real‑time on‑chain payouts via Superfluid, “transparent reward calculations” and analytics, and ML‑based KOL scoring with sentiment/risk analysis and bot detection.
- Why now: influencer marketing and creator economy budgets are expanding; brands are increasingly sensitive to fraud, spam and unclear ROI, creating demand for more measurable rails.

4) MAIN RISKS
===============================================================================
- Smart contract / infra risk:
  - Payouts depend on Base, USDC and the Superfluid protocol; outages or contract issues upstream could impact creator payments.
  - Superfluid introduces additional smart‑contract complexity beyond simple transfers; integration quality and security practices matter.
- Token / incentives risk:
  - No native platform token is clearly described, but campaigns may pay in various tokens; risk depends on the specific reward asset (stablecoin vs volatile token).
  - Stablecoins can experience stress events and temporary de‑pegs; “stable” does not mean risk‑free.
- Business / PMF risk:
  - Influencer marketing platforms are competitive; Kreators must sustain both creator supply and reliable campaign demand.
  - Two‑sided marketplace dynamics mean frustration on either side (brands or creators) can impair growth.
- Centralization and platform dependence:
  - Identity and distribution are tied to X; changes in X policy, APIs or enforcement can affect onboarding and campaign visibility.
  - Verification and operations involve centralized flows (e.g., Telegram verification bots), creating potential bottlenecks.
- Regulatory / compliance risk:
  - Crypto promotions increasingly fall under advertising and financial‑promotion rules; both brands and creators must manage disclosure and jurisdictional constraints.

11) How to evaluate quickly (practical steps)
======================================================================
For brands
1) Try to create a campaign:

   - USDC on Base? Any escrow? Any platform fees?

1) Sign up and connect your X account.
2) Look at campaign terms and rules.
3) Track how rewards are computed (performance-based formula).
4) Verify payouts via your wallet and on Base explorer links.
