STRICT COMMENT GENERATION RULES:

• Each reply must be 7–10 words
• No punctuation at the end
• No emojis or hashtags
• No hype language or clichés
• Avoid these banned words:
  game changer
  finally
  excited
  thrilled
  love to see
  huge
  insane
  amazing
  incredible
  must read
  unbelievable
• Replies must not sound generic or repetitive
• No repeated structures between replies
• Avoid starting lines with:
  this is
  that’s why
  looks like
  feels like
• Tone should be natural, casual, lightly opinionated
